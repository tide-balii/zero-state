## Selected Approach: **Idea C — Zero-Knowledge Vault**
Chosen for its balance of technical credibility and visual polish. The emerald-on-dark theme for employee views communicates security and privacy, while the clean indigo-on-white employer ledger communicates transparency and corporate trust.
